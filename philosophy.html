PCFS Philosophy

What We Believe In

At PCFS we focus on a holistic approach to learning and development with our K-8 students. With the collapse of the past education systems, we find that incorporating a supportive learning environment is crucial to supporting child development in this time of transition.

Our 6 core values at PCFS are diversity, safety, freedom, equality, inclusion and opportunity.

  • Diversity: We believe that immersing students in other cultures and socioeconomic backgrounds grants students a type of intellect other schools may lack in providing.
